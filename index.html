Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the duration of the program?

The program is flexible and designed to accommodate students at various learning levels. Specific schedules and durations will be shared upon registration.

2. Do I need prior knowledge of Quranic recitation to join?

No! Whether you’re starting from the basics or looking to refine your skills, this class caters to all levels.

3. What age group is eligible for the program?

Children of school-going age and sisters of all ages are welcome to join.

4. What do I need to participate in the classes?

You’ll need a stable internet connection, access to WhatsApp or Zoom, and a notebook for taking notes.

5. Is one-on-one tutoring available?

Yes, individual sessions can be arranged upon request. Please inquire for more details.

6. How do I stay updated about class timings and materials?

Updates will be shared regularly through WhatsApp groups and email notifications.
